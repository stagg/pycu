Seite 3

Nokia Scrum Assessment

You’re not doing Agile when…

You’re not doing Scrum when…

• Iterations are > 6 weeks

• Your product backlog doesn't contain estimates

• Iterations are not timeboxed

• You can't generate a release burn-down chart

• Specs try to finish before programming

• You don't know your velocity

• Iteration does not result in workable code

• You don't know the product owner

• Iterations don't include testing
